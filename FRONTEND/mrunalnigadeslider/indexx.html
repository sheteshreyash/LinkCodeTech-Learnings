BLOOD TEST

A laboratory analysis of a person's blood sample to measure various components as red and white blood cells, platelets, electrolytes, glucose, hormones.

X-RAY

X-ray is a medical imaging technique that uses electromagnetic radiation to create images of the inside of the body. Used to diagnose bone fractures, lung infection.

CT SCAN

A procedure that uses a computer linked to an x-ray machine to make a series of detailed pictures of areas inside the body.

SONOGRAPHY

It is a non-invasive medical imaging procedure that uses high-frequency sound waves to create images of the body's internal structures.
